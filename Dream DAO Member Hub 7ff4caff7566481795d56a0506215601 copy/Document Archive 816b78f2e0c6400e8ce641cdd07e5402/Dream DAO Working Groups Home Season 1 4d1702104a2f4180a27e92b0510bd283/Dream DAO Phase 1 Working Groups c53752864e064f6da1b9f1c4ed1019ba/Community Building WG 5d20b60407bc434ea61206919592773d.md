💡 **In a nutshell-**
The Community Building Working Group has been **created** to *foster a strong spirit of community in the Dream DAO where people are excited to plug in, hang out, collaborate, and support each other*. 
This working group has the **responsibility** to *steward and contribute to the processes of* creating the Systems of-
→ Onboarding
→ Replugging
→ Events and Spaces
→ Conflict Resolution

and the Greenpapers-
→ The Dream DAO Constitution
→ Role of Diversity, Equity and Inclusion in Representation, Operation, and Impact Dream DAO Makes
