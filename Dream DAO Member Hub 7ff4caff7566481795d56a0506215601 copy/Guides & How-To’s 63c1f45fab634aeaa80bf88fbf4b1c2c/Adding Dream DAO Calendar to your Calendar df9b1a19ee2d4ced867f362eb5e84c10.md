### Step 2: Subscribe to that calendar

Go to Google Calendar at [https://calendar.google.com/](https://calendar.google.com/)

In Google Calendar...

- Click the settings icon that looks like a gear on the top right
- Click "**Add Calendar**"
- Click "**Subscribe to calendar**"
